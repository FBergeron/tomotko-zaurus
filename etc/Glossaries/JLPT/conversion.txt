To convert this files (found at http://www.thbz.org/kanjimots/jlpt.php3) for toMOTko, I did the following.  It's not perfect yet.  It should be improved :

Extract the extra file and edit it with vim this way :

1) Remove header.
2) :%s/^M//
3) :%s@] /@#
4) :%s@/$@@
5) :%s \[@/
6) :%s@#@/
7) :g/]$/:d
8) :%s@^\(.\{-}\) ,@\1/\1/@
